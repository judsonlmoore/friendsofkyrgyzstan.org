What Is Friends of Kyrgyzstan?

FOK’s mission is to facilitate communication among members and promote exchange of information by responding to requests for and sharing information about Kyrgyzstan and facilitating the sharing and exchange of ideas, activities, materials, resources and expertise among members and others.

We focus on representing the Peace Corps experience and support the continual learning of members in areas related to the culture, country and development of Kyrgyzstan including the promotion and facilitation of projects our members undertake with this aim.

Dialogue

We promote continuous dialogue regarding the work Peace Corps Volunteers have accomplished in Kyrgyzstan by hosting meet ups, online conversations, and representing our cumulative experiences to community and government leaders.

Fundraising

We support projects of current Peace Corps Volunteers in Kyrgyzstan through grants we fund with the generosity of our supporters. We are a registered 501(c)3 organization. Make your tax-deductible donation today.
