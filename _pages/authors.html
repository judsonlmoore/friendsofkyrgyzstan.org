Third Goal through writing

Fullfil the Peace Corps Third Goal by sharing your story with the world on the Friends of Kyrgyzstan blog

Thank you to our wonderful authors! Over the years many PCVs, RPCVs, counterparts, colleagues, and staff have all contributed to the content found on the blog. If you would also like to be a contributing offer, please contact us with a pitch for what you might like to write about.

Topics of focus:

  • Why you wanted to become a Peace Corps Volunteer
  • What is the legacy you left behind with your work?
  • What advice would you give to others about Peace Corps or Kyrgyzstan?

We are eager to hear your story soon!
